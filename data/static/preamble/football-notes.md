--- Football Notes ---
Match minutes: 45' means "in the 45th minute" (clock shows 44:xx).
  Added time: 45'+3' means 3rd minute of added time after the 45th.
Pitch: 105m long, 68m wide. Penalty area extends 16.5m from goal line.
  6-yard box extends 5.5m. Centre circle radius 9.15m.
Positions: GK = goalkeeper, DF = defender, MF = midfielder, FW = forward.
This is association football (soccer). Two halves of 45 minutes.
  Added time at the end of each half for stoppages.
