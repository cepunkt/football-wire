## Stakes
Both teams drew their openers. A win puts the winner in pole position
for qualification. A draw keeps everything congested. A loss makes
MD3 a must-win.

## Key Matchups
- **De Bruyne (#7 BEL) vs Iran's defensive block**: Belgium's creator
  vs Iran's organization. If Iran sit deep and compact — as they
  will — De Bruyne needs to find the pass that breaks the lines.
  He couldn't do it against Egypt.
- **Lukaku (#9 BEL) vs Khalilzadeh/Kanaanizadegan**: Iran's
  experienced centre-backs (37 and 32) vs Belgium's powerful striker.
  Physical battle in the box.
- **Taremi (#9 IRN) vs Debast/Theate**: Iran's biggest threat on the
  counter. If Taremi holds the ball up and brings others into play,
  Iran can hurt Belgium's high line.
- **Beiranvand (#1 IRN) vs Belgium's set pieces**: The GK who saved
  Ronaldo's penalty in 2018. Big-game experience. Belgium's aerial
  threat from De Bruyne's delivery is their best route.

## Prediction Territory (campfire)
Belgium should have enough quality, but their MD1 performance against
Egypt was concerning. Iran will be organized, physical, and dangerous
on the counter. Belgium 1-0 or 2-1 feels most likely, but Iran
grinding a draw is very possible. Iran don't lose big at World Cups —
they make you earn everything.
