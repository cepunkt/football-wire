## Pre-Match Context
Ecuador vs Curacao | Kansas City (Arrowhead Stadium) | 2026-06-21

First ever meeting between these nations. Both lost their openers —
Ecuador 0-1 to Ivory Coast (Diallo 90'), Curacao 1-7 to Germany.
Both need a result to keep qualification hopes alive.

## Stakes
Germany qualified with 6 pts. CIV on 3 pts in strong position.
- **ECU win**: moves to 3 pts, level with CIV. MD3 (ECU vs GER) becomes
  a chance to overtake CIV on GD or secure 3rd place.
- **CUW win**: massive upset, moves to 3 pts. Would face CIV in MD3
  with qualification still possible.
- **Draw**: 1 pt each. CIV almost certainly through. Both ECU and CUW
  would need results in MD3 AND other groups to cooperate for 3rd place.
  CUW's -6 GD makes this nearly impossible for them.

## Key Matchups
- **Caicedo (#23 ECU) vs Bacuna brothers (#7, #10 CUW)**: Midfield
  battle. Caicedo is Chelsea's destroyer — physical, aggressive,
  covers ground. The Bacunas are technical and clever but less
  powerful. Control of midfield likely decides this.
- **Hincapie/Pacho vs Comenencia/Chong**: Ecuador's world-class
  centre-back pairing against Curacao's most dangerous attackers.
  Comenencia showed against Germany he can finish. Chong has the
  technical quality to create.
- **Ecuador's set pieces**: Torres (#2), Hincapie (#3), Pacho (#6)
  are all 185cm+. Curacao will struggle aerially. Dead balls could
  be decisive.

## Head to Head
No previous meetings. First ever encounter.

## Venue
Arrowhead Stadium, Kansas City. Capacity 73,000. Home of the NFL's
Kansas City Chiefs. Natural grass surface. Altitude ~250m (no factor).
Central timezone — evening kickoff, manageable conditions.

## Prediction Territory (campfire)
Ecuador should have too much quality defensively. The question is
whether Beccacece's system can generate enough going forward. A
narrow Ecuador win (1-0, 2-0) feels most likely. But Curacao showed
against Germany they can threaten on the counter — one moment of
quality could change everything.
