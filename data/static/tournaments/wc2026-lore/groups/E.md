## Group Narrative
Germany's opening 7-1 demolition of Curacao set the tone, but the
real story was Ivory Coast's 90th-minute robbery of Ecuador. Amad
Diallo — Manchester United's winger — scored the latest winner of
the group stage.

Germany's Matchday 2 against CIV was a reality check: Fofana (GK)
was extraordinary, Kessie put CIV ahead, and it took Nagelsmann's
aggressive triple substitution at 60' to turn it around. Undav's
super-sub brace (68', 90'+4') rescued Germany from what would have
been a genuine crisis.

The group dynamic: Germany and CIV have the quality. Ecuador have the
system but lack goals. Curacao have the story but face a brutal
quality gap. ECU vs CUW is a must-win for both — a draw likely
eliminates them.
