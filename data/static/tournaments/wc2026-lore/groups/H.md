## Group Narrative
The group of shocks. Spain held by Cape Verde. Uruguay held by Saudi
Arabia. The two heavyweights stumbled simultaneously. Neither looked
like the dominant force their ranking suggests.

Spain's 0-0 is the bigger shock — Euro 2024 champions with Yamal,
Rodri, Pedri, Nico Williams, and they couldn't score against a
nation of 600,000 people. Vozinha at 40 years old keeping a clean
sheet against the European champions. It's the kind of result that
makes you question everything or question nothing — maybe Cape Verde
are simply that well-organized.

Saudi Arabia leading Uruguay for 39 minutes feels like 2022 revisited.
The template works: organized block, set piece threat, counter-attack.
Al-Amri's header showed the method. Bielsa's pressing was frustrated.

Tomorrow's matches are pivotal: Spain vs Saudi Arabia is the clash of
the upsets — Saudi who beat Argentina, Spain who drew with Cape Verde.
Both need a win. Uruguay vs Cape Verde is the reality check — can
Cape Verde sustain their defensive miracle against Bielsa's relentless
intensity?
