## Group Narrative
The group of narratives. Messi's farewell vs Rangnick's revolution.
Alaba's 2-0 career record against Messi. Cordoba 1978 echoes.
Argentina's quest to defend the title vs Austria's quest to prove
they belong at the top table.

Messi's hat trick against Algeria was the tournament's first truly
individual performance — three goals, each clinical, each inevitable.
At 39, he's not running past defenders. He's making the ball do the
work. The hat trick was his first ever at a World Cup. Some things
take until 39 to happen.

Austria's win over Jordan was messier — Schmid's historic goal broke
28 years of silence, but Jordan equalized and Austria needed an own
goal and a 90'+12' penalty to make it 3-1. Rangnick's pressing was
effective but the killing instinct wasn't there.

Monday's ARG vs AUT is the group's defining match. A win for either
likely secures qualification. Austria can dream — Alaba has never
lost to Messi, and Rangnick's pressing could trouble Argentina's
build-up. But Argentina are Argentina, and Messi is Messi.
