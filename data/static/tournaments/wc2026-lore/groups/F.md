## Group Narrative
The group of wild scorelines. NED 2-2 JPN was the tactical battle.
Everything else has been carnage — SWE 5-1 TUN, NED 5-1 SWE. Goals
everywhere.

Netherlands are through and look dangerous. Brobbey's emergence as
a clinical striker changes their profile from 2022. Gakpo still
delivers on the big stage. Summerville has scored in both matches.

Sweden's position is paradoxical: beat Tunisia 5-1, then lost to
Netherlands by the same score. They can play but the defensive
fragility against top opposition is real. Potter's rebuild is
attacking but leaky.

Japan are the quiet threat. Drew with a Netherlands side that then
put 5 past Sweden. Moriyasu's tactical flexibility and late-game
composure (Kamada 88') suggest a team that grows into tournaments.
2022 echoes everywhere.

Tunisia's story is the coaching drama. Lamouchi sacked after one
game, Renard parachuted in. Can the man who beat Argentina with Saudi
Arabia work another miracle? The squad has talent (Mejbri, Skhiri,
young prospects) but the psychological damage of 5-1 is real.
