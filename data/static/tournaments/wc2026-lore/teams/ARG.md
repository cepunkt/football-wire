# Argentina (ARG)

## Tournament History
19th World Cup appearance. 3x winners (1978, 1986, 2022). Six finals
total (runners-up 1930, 1990, 2014). DEFENDING CHAMPIONS. Copa America holders too. The most decorated active
national team. Messi's 2022 coronation in Qatar completed football's
greatest individual story. Now he's back at 39 for one more.

## Current Squad
Coach: Lionel Scaloni (ARG). Won the 2022 World Cup and 2024 Copa
America. Quiet, tactical, trusts his players. Built the most
cohesive Argentina team in decades.
Assistants: Pablo Aimar (ARG — childhood idol of Messi), Roberto
Ayala (ARG — legendary CB), Walter Samuel (ARG — legendary CB).
The coaching staff is a 2006 Argentina reunion.

System: 4-3-3 / 4-4-2. Flexible, pragmatic, built around Messi's
freedom. The team defends collectively and attacks through individual
brilliance. 17 of the 2022 World Cup winners recalled to this squad.

Key players: Lionel Messi (#10, FW) Inter Miami — turns 39 on June
24 mid-tournament. The GOAT. Hat trick against Algeria — 16 WC goals,
tying Klose's all-time record. Still scoring. Still creating. This
is the farewell. Julian Alvarez (#9, FW) Atletico Madrid — the successor.
26, clinical, tireless. Enzo Fernandez (#24, MF) Chelsea — the 2022
breakout star, now established. Rodrigo De Paul (#7, MF) Inter
Miami — Messi's bodyguard and midfield partner.

Atletico Madrid bloc: Musso, Alvarez, N. Gonzalez, Almada, Giuliano
Simeone, Molina — SIX Atletico players. Giuliano Simeone (#17) is
Diego Simeone's son.

Emiliano Martinez (#23, GK) Aston Villa — the penalty specialist,
the World Cup final hero. When it matters most, Dibu delivers.

Cristian Romero (#13, DF) Tottenham — the aggressive CB who defines
the defensive line. Lisandro Martinez (#6, DF) Man United — the
other warrior CB.

Nico Paz (#18, FW) Como — born September 2004, 21. Argentine-Spanish
dual national who chose Argentina. The next generation.

## Storylines
- Messi's farewell tour: 39 years old, at Inter Miami, and he's
  still scoring World Cup hat tricks. This is the last World Cup.
  The entire tournament is framed as Messi's goodbye.
- 17 of the 2022 winners are back: Scaloni recalled the core that
  won in Qatar. The continuity is the strength — these players know
  exactly how to win a World Cup.
- Simeone's son: Giuliano Simeone wearing the Argentina shirt at a
  World Cup. His father Diego is one of Argentina's most iconic
  players. The dynasty continues.
- Alvarez as the heir: when Messi finally stops, Alvarez takes the
  crown. At Atletico Madrid, he's already shown he can carry a team.
- The hat trick: Messi's hat trick vs Algeria was his first in a
  World Cup. At 39. Some things don't need explaining.

## Observed Patterns
- Messi freedom: the system creates space for Messi to operate. When
  he's on the ball, everything stops and waits.
- Defensive solidity: clean sheet vs Algeria. Romero and Martinez
  are elite.
- Clinical finishing: 3 goals from clinical chances. No waste.
- Depth: Alvarez, Almada, Paz, Lo Celso, Lautaro Martinez — the
  attacking options are absurd.
