# Austria (AUT)
WC history: 8 appearances. Best: 3rd place (1954).
Coach: Ralf Rangnick (GER)
Style: Gegenpressing, high-energy, structured system. Salzburg academy pipeline.
Key players: David Alaba (DF), Marcel Sabitzer (MF), Konrad Laimer (MF), Romano Schmid (MF)
Group J: Argentina, Algeria, Jordan
Narrative: First WC goal in 28 years (Schmid, 20'). Beat Jordan 3-1. Rangnick built from academy up. 23 of 26 players play abroad. Alaba 2-0 vs Messi career record. Córdoba 1978 lives forever.
