# Croatia (CRO)
WC history: 7 appearances. Best: Runners-up (2018). 3rd (2022).
Coach: Zlatko Dalić (CRO)
Style: Midfield excellence, tournament resilience, never-say-die mentality.
Key players: Luka Modrić (MF/C), Josko Gvardiol (DF), Mateo Kovacic (MF), Martin Baturina (MF)
Group L: England, Ghana, Panama
Narrative: Lost 2-4 to England but equalized TWICE (Baturina, Musa 45+5'). Modrić subbed off after giving away penalty — possibly his last WC. Only 2 WC losses ever, both 4-2.
